# Groups

Collections of people that act or are reasoned about as a unit: teams, departments, market segments, customer cohorts, boards, committees, user groups.

## What belongs here

One note per group. Use `type: group` with `subtype:` one of: `team`, `department`, `segment`, `cohort`, `board`, `committee`, `user_group`. Link member `People/` notes and the parent `Entity` (the organization the group belongs to).

A group is the right home when membership and collective behavior matter; an individual is `People/`; a whole organization or business unit treated as an instrument is `Entities/`.

## Mutability

Mutable hub. Membership, mandate, and composition change over time — update in place and bump `updated:`. Significant claims about what a group did or decided belong in `Findings/` or `Decisions/`.

## Sensitivity

Group composition often reveals personal affiliations, so treat at least as `internal`, and `confidential` when membership or segment definitions are non-public. Customer cohorts derived from data inherit the data's sensitivity — classify up. No secrets in the body.
